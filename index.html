FAQ
Common
questions

Do guests need to download anything?

No. Guests scan the QR with their phone camera and the portal opens instantly in their browser. No app, no login, no password — ever.

How do staff receive notifications?

Staff receive a push notification and audio chime via OneSignal — even when the browser is in the background or the screen is locked.

Can I manage multiple hotels?

Yes. One owner account can manage multiple hotels — each with separate branding, rooms, staff, HODs and analytics. Switch between hotels in one tap.

How does the HOD system work?

Each department head gets a unique Hotel Code + Name + PIN login. They see only their department's requests, manage their own staff, and customise guest options.

How long does setup take?

Under 30 minutes. Sign up, add your hotel details, add rooms, download QR codes, add staff PINs. No IT team, no installation required.

Can HODs add custom guest options?

Yes. Each HOD can toggle the default options for their department on or off, and add any custom service they want guests to see — like "Live Music Info" or "Rooftop Access".
