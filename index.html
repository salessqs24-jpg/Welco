FAQ
Common questions

Do guests need to download anything?

No. Guests scan the QR code with their phone camera and the portal opens instantly in their browser. No app, no account, no password.

How do staff receive notifications?

Staff get a push notification and a chime sound on their Android phone — even when the browser is in the background or the screen is locked.

Can I use it for multiple hotels?

Yes. Each hotel has its own account, branding, rooms, staff and data. DownTown and BambooSaa are completely separate on the platform.

How long does setup take?

Under 30 minutes. Sign up, add your rooms, download QR codes, add your staff PINs, and you're live. No IT support needed.

What if a guest's request is urgent?

The SLA timer turns red after 15 minutes and staff see it highlighted urgently. Push notifications fire immediately when a request is submitted.

Can I post notices to all guests?

Yes. The Announcements feature lets you post a message that guests see as a banner the moment they open their room portal.
